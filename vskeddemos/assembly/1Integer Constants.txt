Integer Constants(or Integer literal) = Is made up of an optional leading sign,
one or more digits,and an optional suffix character(called a radix)
indicating the number base.

Radix may be one of the following(uppercase or lowercase)
h Hexadecimal (0-9,A-F),begin with "0x" or "0X" followed by one or more hexadecimal digits
q/o Octal (0-7)
d Decimal (0-9)
b Binary (0,1)
r Encoded real
t Decimal(alternate) (0-9)
y Binary(alternate) (0,1)


Examples of using different radixes:

26 Decimal
26d Decimal
11010011b Binary
42q Octal
42o Octal
1Ah Hexadecimal
0A3h Hexadecimal
